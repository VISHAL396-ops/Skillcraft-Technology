Scroll Down to See the Effect

The navigation bar at the top is currently transparent. As you scroll down this page, it will turn into a solid color and a subtle shadow will appear.

Additionally, if you hover your mouse over any of the menu items, their color will change and an underline will animate in. This is all achieved with a combination of CSS for styling and a small amount of JavaScript to detect the scroll action.
